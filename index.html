As teenagers looking to learn the ends and outs of the stock market, we found it very difficult to obtain the basic skills and habits necessary to trade successfully in a commuity dominated largely by experienced adults. Most of the trading servers and forums we joined were filled with knowledgeable members who knew exactly what they were doing and found it a waste of time to mentor a newer trader. In addition ,instructors were out of the question because of the large tuition fee that kids without a job simply could not afford. We knew there had to be a better way for young investors to learn.
In response to the lack of resources available to us, we decided to create a community full of teens just like us so that we could teach and even learn alongside them. Investing Ahead was established in order for anyone, regardless of age, to learn how to invest and trade in the stock market.

Education

We provide resources for you to become a better investor or maybe even just get started.

Competition

We hold friendly competition among our community such as stock market games where participants can earn cool prizes.

Discussion

Investing Ahead can also just be a place for people like us to get in touch with each other and work together to become better.

Guidance

Everyone in the communbity can contribute to plays and watchlists that we put out to help you while trading.

Investing Ahead

A student-run organization that provides fellow students with the necessary resources to learn trading fundamentals.
