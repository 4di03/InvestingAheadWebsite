About the Founders

Kevin Zhang and Adithya Palle

My name is Kevin Zhang and I am currently a Senior at American High School in Fremont, CA. I started investing in crypto and options in Freshman year and I wanted to share the knowledge I had gained since then, so I proposed to found this project. The first thing I found out when attempting to do research on the stock market was that there were a plethora of resources for me to use, but I did not find every single YouTube video or trading article useful and often times I sat through an hour-long video without learning much about the exact topic I wanted to understand. I took it upon myself to condense this broad range of resources so that I could make it easier for other students like me with an interest in finance to get started and be there to provide personalized support in a more relaxed environment, a luxury I did not have when I tried to get into trading.

I'm Adithya, one of the founders of Investing Ahead. My motivation to start Investing Ahead began in the summer of my freshman year of high school. I would trade options on my robinhood account but really had no idea what I was doing. I sought help, but no one was really willing to lend a hand to a 14-year-old trader like me. Being tired of this, I got together with my friend who is also interested in investing, Kevin, and we decided to make a community where we could trade with other teens like ourselves. I also decided I would be the one to make the website for it. Bringing together my interest in coding and finance, I worked together with Kevin to start Investing Ahead. I'm really grateful to all our staff and members who allowed us to make a community that was unavailable to me previously.
